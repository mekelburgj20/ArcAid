Welcome to the ArcAid Server!

Welcome! This server is the home base for ArcAid, a tournament management platform for virtual pinball, retro gaming, and beyond. ArcAid automates your iScored.info tournaments end-to-end so you can focus on playing.

What ArcAid Does

Hands-Free Tournament Rotations: Automatically locks finished games, determines winners, activates the next game in the lineup, and posts announcements to your tournament's Discord channel.

Winner Picks: Winners earn the right to pick the next game. If they don't pick in time, it falls to the runner-up. If nobody picks, ArcAid selects one automatically from the game library.

Multi-Tournament Support: Run daily, weekly, monthly, or custom tournaments simultaneously, each with their own schedule, game lineup, platform rules, and cleanup settings.

Platform-Aware Game Library: Maintain a shared library of games across platforms (VPXS, VR, FX3, etc.). Tournaments can require or exclude specific platforms so only eligible games are picked.

Player Stats and Leaderboards: Tracks wins, scores, and play history. View stats in Discord or on the public web scoreboard.

Admin UI: A full web dashboard for managing tournaments, the game library, scores, backups, settings, and more.

Player Commands

/submit-score - Submit your score along with a validation photo.
/list-scores - View the current leaderboard for any active game.
/list-active - See which games are currently live across all tournaments.
/list-winners - See recent tournament winners.
/my-stats - View your personal win count, scores, and game history.
/pick-game - Won a tournament? Use this to choose the next game.
/view-selection - See what's queued up and available to pick from.
/nominate-picker - Pass your picking rights to another player.
/map-user - Link your Discord account to your iScored username.
