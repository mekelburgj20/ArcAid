Max 10
This format awards points to players based on their rank on each game, but only to the top 10 players for each game.   Their 25 best games are factored into their total score.   Points are awarded as follows:
1st: 100 points
2nd: 80 points
3rd: 65 points
4th: 50 points
5th: 40 points
6th: 30 points
7th: 20 points
8th: 15 points
9th: 10 points
10th: 5 points

Average Rank
This ranks players based on their average position on each game's leaderboard.   The calculation will be based on each player's 25 best games, and they must have submitted scores for at least 25 games in order to be included in the calculation.

Best Game (PAPA)
This format awards points to players based on their rank on each game.   Their 25 best games are factored into their total score.   Points are awarded as follows:
1st: 100 points
2nd: 90 points
3rd: 85 points
Each subsequent place is worth 1 point less.

Best Game (Linear)
This format awards points to players based on their rank on each game.   Their 25 best games are factored into their total score.   Points are awarded as follows:
1st: 100 points
2nd: 99 points
3rd: 98 points
Each subsequent place is worth 1 point less.
